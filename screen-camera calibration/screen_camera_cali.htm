Coming Soon

 

This website will be used to promote the screen-camera calibration algorithm introduced in the following paper

 

Songnan Li, King Ngi Ngan, Lu Sheng, ``Screen-camera calibration using a thread``, submitted to ICIP 2014

 

Depending on the acceptance of the paper, the website will publish (1) a general introduction of the screen-camera calibration, (2) a MATLAB code of the proposed algorithm, (3) a video demonstrating the usage of the code, and (4) a video demonstrating one of the applications, i.e., holographic-like displaying.
