Welcome to my homepage! I am a Research Assistant Professor in the Department of Electronic Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, with Prof. King Ngi NGAN.

Research Interests: screen-camera calibration, head tracking, free-viewpoint video, visual quality assessment, anaglyph, image and video compression, video de-interlacing, code optimization, etc.
